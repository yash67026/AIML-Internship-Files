1. Google Search
   Uses ML models to rank web pages by relevance based on past searches, keywords, and user behavior.

2. YouTube Recommendations
   ML analyzes watch history, likes, and viewing time to suggest videos similar to user interests.

3. Smartphone Face Unlock
   Uses deep learning to recognize facial features and match them with stored facial data.

4. Google Maps Navigation
   ML predicts traffic conditions using historical data and real-time location information from users.

5. Email Spam Filter (Gmail)
   ML classifies emails as spam or safe by learning from previously labeled emails.

6. Voice Assistants (Google Assistant / Siri)
   Speech recognition models convert voice to text and NLP models understand user commands.

7. Autocorrect & Predictive Text
   ML predicts the next word or correct spelling based on language patterns and past typing behavior.

8. Netflix Recommendations
   ML studies viewing history and compares it with similar users to suggest movies and series.

9. Online Shopping Suggestions (Amazon / Flipkart)
   Recommendation systems use ML to analyze browsing, purchases, and ratings to suggest products.

10. Social Media Feed (Instagram / X)
    ML ranks posts by predicting what the user is most likely to engage with (likes, comments, shares).
